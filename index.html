Accreditation

CareFlight of the Rockies is proud to be accredited by the Commission on Accreditation of Medical Transport Systems. Developed exclusively by and for air medical services, this national accreditation process focusses on the patient care quality and safe transport through an in-depth review by professional surveyors of the commission.

Who is CareFlight of the Rockies?

Emergency medicine physicians, cardiologists. Pulmonologists, trauma surgeons, neurosurgeons, cardiovascular surgeons, neurologists, perinatologists, neonatologists, obstetricians, and other specialty physicians in contact with the CareFlight of the Rocies team


Specialty Teams

• Adult/Pediatric: Flight Nurse/Flight Paramedic
• Neonatal: Flight Neonatal Nurse Practitioner/ Flight Neonatal Nurse or Flight Nurse/Paramedic
• Obstetrical: Flight Obstetrical Nurse and Flight Nurse/Paramedic

CareFlight of the Rockies
